safe space is a portal for people who feel currently unsafe and are unable to contact anyone directly. it gives them the opportunity to talk to other people who feel unsafe to verify their status, and keep a log of their location.
to use safe space, first head over the the "unsafe" page. then, enter in your personal information as well as a secret to verify your identity. feel free to use the chat to speak to other people live, or fill out the unsafe page again to keep it updated of your location. once you are safe, scroll down to the safe button and use your secret to remove your information from the "currently unsafe" table.
